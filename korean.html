Hi! Ready to learn some Korean?
    Korean can be divided to 2 big groups; formal and casual.
    Because most of you will be using these sentences to speak to strangers, I'm going to teach you the formal way.
    This will make most sentences end with -yo.
    If you're not sure if you're being formal you could add -yo at the end.
    (It may be grammarly incorrect, but hey, you're trying.)
    So, lets begin with some simple sayings.
안녕하세요 Annyeong-ha-se-yo Hello. / How are you?
감사합니다 Gamsa-hamnida Thank you
죄송합니다 Joesong-hamnida I'm sorry
안녕히 계세요 Annyeonghi ga-se-yo Goodbye(when you're staying and the person is leaving)
안녕히 가세요 Annyeonghi gye-se-yo Goodbye(when you're leaving and the person is staying)
저기요 Jeogi-yo excuse me(when you're trying to grab somenone's attention)
잠시만요 Jamshimanyo Wait a minute(you can also use this when someone is blocking your way, sort of like excuse me.)
-주세요 (n) Ju-se-yo Please give me (n)
네 Ne Yes
아니요 A-ni-yo No
몰라요 Mollayo I don't know
알아요 Ara-yo I know
괜찮아요 Gwaen-chan-a-yo That's fine/That's ok/I'm fine
환영합니다 Hwan-yeong hamnida Welcome
좋아요 Jo-a-yo Good
안 좋아요 An jo-a-yo Bad
사랑해요 Sarang-hae-yo I love you 
 Okay! Now for some sentences you can use while traveling.
얼마에요? Eol-ma-ye-yo? How much is it?
영어 하시나요? Yung-uh ha-shi-na-yo? Can you speak English?
영어 할 수 있어요. Yung-uh hal su eet-suh-yo. I speak English(If you make the end note higher;making it a question mark, it means: Do you speak English?)
화장실 어디예요? Hwa-jang-shil uh-di-eh-yo? Where is the bathroom?
저 (땅콩) 알러지 있어요. Juh (ddang-kong) al-luh-ji eet-suh-yo. I have an (peanut) allergy.(I'm sure you can pronounce allergy in your own pronunciation but you may have to sound it a Korean way for people to understand.)
좋은 하루 보내세요! Jo-eun haru bo-nae-se-yo Have a nice day!
다시 말해 주세요 Dashi mal-hae ju-se-yo Please repeat that
천천히 말해 주세요 Cheoncheon-hi malhae jus-se-yo Please speak slowly
이게 뭐에요? i-ge mwoye-yo? What is this?
걱정 하지 마세요! Geokjeong haji ma-se-yo! Don't worry!
문제 없어요 Mun-je eopseoyo No worries
얼마나 매워요? Ul-mana maewoyo? How spicy is it?
맵지 않게 요리해 주세요 Maebji anke yorihae ju-se-yo Please do not make this spicy
소주 한 병 주세요 Soju hanbyeong ju-se-yo Please give me 1 bottle of soju
와이파이 있어요? waipai eet-suh-yo? Do you have wifi here?
비밀번호 뭐에요? bimil bunho meo-e-yo? What is the password?
